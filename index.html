    Best fonts every designer

    Every designer relies on timeless fonts that balance readability, versatility, and style. Classics like Helvetica, Futura, Garamond, Baskerville, and Gill Sans remain staples for clean, professional work. Modern picks like Montserrat, Poppins, and Inter dominate digital design for their legibility on screens. For creativity, Lora, Playfair Display, and Bebas Neue are favorites in branding and editorial projects. The best designers know: choose fonts that match the message, ensure visual hierarchy, and never overuse too many in one project.

    The forgotten art of spacing

    The forgotten art of spacing is about recognizing whitespace as a vital design element, not wasted space. Proper spacing creates balance, clarity, and flow, allowing users to focus without feeling overwhelmed. Margins, padding, and line height guide the eye and improve readability, while consistent grid systems provide harmony. Ignoring spacing leads to cluttered, stressful interfaces. Great designers know that restraint—leaving room to breathe—often speaks louder than adding more elements. Spacing transforms design from functional to elegant, improving usability and aesthetics.
